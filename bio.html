About Me

Hi! I'm Woohyun, but everyone calls me Gordon. I'm a mechanical engineering student based in Canada, currently enrolled at the University of Toronto. I'm also minoring in robotics and mechatronics.

I created this website to share my university projects and notes. Over time, it has evolved into a resource for students and anyone curious to learn.

My research interests include the applications of ceramics in defense, autonomous systems, active protection systems, and energy systems related to hypersonic flight. Outside of work, I enjoy listening to podcasts, hiking, experimenting with new technology, snapping photos, and drafting designs I hope to bring to life.

Thanks for stopping by! "Oh, and in case I don't see you, good afternoon, good evening and good night!"
