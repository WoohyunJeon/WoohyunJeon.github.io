Who am I?

My name is Gordon and I'm currently an engineering student at the University of Toronto Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering. This website was originally developed to simply store my university notes & projects but due to the interest shown by my peers and professors, I've decided to develop it to serve as a survival guide for oncoming students or just a space for those curious to learn something new.

I strive to make the content I post educational and entertaining by focusing on presentation and making the narrative engaging and I also hope it will help you appreciate and discover the joy I have in learning.

The majority of the content you see will remain free and open source. All my course notes, projects, files, and tutorials will be available for anyone to use in the hopes it supplements you in the learning process.

Happy Reading,
